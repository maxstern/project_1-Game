Mine Field will test your memory.

You must navigate the horse to it's home without riding it into any mines

The mines will be visible, and you must memorize where they are. When you
are ready, press 'READY' (or the space bar), and the mines will become
invisible. Navigate the horse with the arrow keys.

The number of mines will keep increasing until you lose. Good luck!
